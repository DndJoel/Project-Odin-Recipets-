Chicken Gyros

Chicken gyros is a popular Greek dish made with marinated chicken, typically cooked on a vertical rotisserie. The seasoned chicken is thinly sliced and served in a pita bread wrap.   • 1 (1/2 cups) chopped fresh thyme
